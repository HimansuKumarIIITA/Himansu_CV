Welcome

I am Dr. Himansu Kumar, a research scientist specializing in bioinformatics and computational biology. With expertise in WGS, RNA-Seq, computational drug discovery, and structural biology, my research focuses on developing cutting-edge tools and databases like FusionNeoAntigen and KinaseFusionDB to unravel the molecular mechanisms of diseases. I am deeply passionate about advancing cancer genomics, targeted therapeutics, and integrating AI for precision medicine.
